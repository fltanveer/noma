Knowledge
Are treatments safe?

Yes, all treatments are administered by NMC-registered nurses. A comprehensive medical screening is mandated before any procedure to ensure physiological compatibility.

How long does an infusion take?

Standard intravenous drip sessions require 45 to 60 minutes. Direct vitamin intramuscular injections (e.g., pure B12) require only a few minutes of your time.

What are your coverage areas?

We provide exclusive mobile concierge services within Zones 1-3 of Greater London. Arrangements for outer zones can be organized upon special request.

Is there pain involved?

We utilize ultra-fine pediatric-grade needles. Most clients feel only a minor, split-second sensation. We also offer cold spray numbing for complete comfort.
