Frequently Asked

How long does the treatment take?

Most IV infusions take between 45 to 60 minutes. Vitamin injections like B12 or Vitamin D take just a few minutes. Our nurse will stay with you throughout the session to ensure your comfort.

Is it safe?

Yes, all our treatments are administered by qualified, registered nurses. We perform a full medical screening before every session to ensure that the chosen infusion is perfectly suited for you.

Where do you provide services?

We are a 100% mobile service covering Greater London. We provide treatments in private homes, offices, hotels, and even at exclusive events.
